Hi, I'm Jieun!

I am a Research Intern at Korea Institute of Science and Technology, working on developing neurocomputing algorithms for enhanced representation building of sensor data. My research focuses on Representation Learning, Brain-Inspired Networks, and Computational Networks.

I received my M.S. in Industrial and Management Engineering from Korea University, South Korea, where I worked with Professor Jungeol Baek. I also hold a B.S. in Statistics and Computer Science from Sookmyung Women's University.

My research interests span anomaly detection systems, multivariate time-series analysis, and machine learning applications in various domains including healthcare and automotive systems.
